About GameTradeX

Our Story

Founded in 2020, GameTradeX started as a small platform for PUBG Mobile players to trade their accounts safely. Recognizing the need for a secure marketplace in the mobile gaming community, we expanded to include other popular games like eFootball, Free Fire, and Call of Duty Mobile.

Today, we're proud to be one of the most trusted platforms for buying and selling mobile game accounts, with thousands of successful transactions and a growing community of gamers worldwide.

How It Works

1

List or Browse

Sellers list their verified accounts with detailed descriptions. Buyers browse our extensive marketplace.

2

3

Enjoy Your Account

Buyers receive their new account details, and sellers get paid. Our support team is always available if needed.
